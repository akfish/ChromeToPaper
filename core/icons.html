Submitting an Icon
If you'd like to submit an icon, create an issue on GitHub. You may also include a message of up to 75 characters to credit yourself for the icon.

The image must be in either PSD or PNG format. It must be square with a minimum size of 128x128. In addition, you must own the rights to the image.

By submitting the image, you are giving jamesmkwan.com permission to modify and use the image as seen fit. Your icon may or may not be used at jamesmkwan.com's sole discretion.
